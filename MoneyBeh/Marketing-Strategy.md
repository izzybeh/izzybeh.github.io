## 📐 Homepage Structure (B+C Hybrid)

### **Phase 1: TRANSFORMATION (Emotional Hook)**
Hook with the outcome, not the process.

**Purpose:** Create desire, relieve anxiety  
**Emotion:** Hope, relief, clarity  
**Message:** "Stop counting dollars. Start living years."

### **Phase 2: EMPATHY (Problem Recognition)**
Validate their pain - the tools are broken, not them.

**Purpose:** Build trust through understanding  
**Emotion:** Validation, recognition  
**Message:** "You're not broken. Financial tools are."

### **Phase 3: METHODOLOGY (Light Touch)**
Show the simple path without overwhelming.

**Purpose:** Simplify the journey  
**Emotion:** Simplicity, possibility  
**Message:** "Two steps: Choose your joy. Choose your time."

### **Phase 4: INSIGHTS (Rational Proof)**
Prove the transformation with unique features.

**Purpose:** Differentiate with concrete value  
**Emotion:** Confidence, curiosity  
**Message:** "Here's what you can't get anywhere else"

### **Phase 5: OUTCOME (Transformation Delivered)**
Close with belonging and commitment.

**Purpose:** Convert to action  
**Emotion:** Belonging, commitment  
**Message:** "Clear decisions. Less stress. More freedom."

## 🎨 Section Details

**Headline:** "Joy today. Freedom tomorrow."  
**Subheadline:** "The only financial tool that shows you when you can make work optional—and when you can work just for joy."  
**CTA:** "See your freedom timeline"

**Key Elements:**

- Single, clear CTA

---

### **2. Problem Section**
**Headline:** "Financial tools are broken"

**The Problems:**
- ❌ Budgeting apps make you feel bad about spending
- ❌ Investment apps feel disconnected from daily life  
- ❌ FIRE calculators are overwhelming spreadsheets
- ❌ Red arrows create shame, not clarity

**Messaging:**
"You're not bad with money. The tools just weren't built for humans."

**Emotion Target:** Validation ("Finally, someone gets it")

---

### **3. Solution Section (2-Step Framework)**
**Headline:** "The MoneyBeh way: Two steps to freedom"

#### **Step 1: Choose Your Joy**
**Icon:** 💭 or ✨  
**Message:** "Build a sustainable life. Focus on what matters. Think in time, not money."

**Key Points:**
- Split your money: Essentials + Joy + Savings
- Reduce wasted spending without deprivation
- Create a lifestyle you can afford forever
- Zero-based budget done with calm

#### **Step 2: Choose Your Time**
**Icon:** ⏰ or 🛤️  
**Message:** "See your clear path. Set it on auto-pilot. Watch compounding work."

**Key Points:**
- See when work becomes optional
- Know when you can work just for joy
- Clear monthly savings targets (bridge + retirement)
- Let compounding do the magic

**Emotion Target:** Simplicity, possibility ("I can do this")

---

### **4. Insights Section (4 Features)**
**Headline:** "Here's what you can't get anywhere else"

Frame features as INSIGHTS, not utilities.

#### **Feature 1: Intentionality Check**
**Tagline:** "Pause before you buy"  
**Copy:** "Is this aligned with what matters? Make conscious choices, not impulse purchases."  
**Belief Alignment:** #4 (Essentialism), #3 (No anxiety)  
**Color:** Seafoam (Do This)

#### **Feature 2: Joy Buckets**
**Tagline:** "Fund what matters, savor the memories"  
**Copy:** "Think in time, not money. See how you're investing your stored time in moments that matter."  
**Belief Alignment:** #2 (Money = stored time), #5 (Depth of moments)  
**Color:** Deep Teal (Learn This)

#### **Feature 3: Freedom Timeline**
**Tagline:** "See when work becomes optional"  
**Copy:** "Two ages that matter: When you can work for joy. When work is optional. Adjust your savings and watch them transform."  
**Belief Alignment:** #1 (Built for humans), #2 (Time over money)  
**Color:** Deep Teal (Learn This)

**Key Clarification:**
- NOT just "years to retirement"
- Shows TWO milestones:
  - **Coast FI:** When you can work for joy (investments cover retirement, just need to cover today)
  - **Full FI:** When work is optional (all expenses covered)
- Interactive: Adjust savings rate, see ages update in real-time
- Motivation through clarity and control

#### **Feature 4: Coast Mode**
**Tagline:** "You're already ahead"  
**Copy:** "If you never saved another dollar, your investments will carry you to freedom. You've already banked years of future freedom."  
**Belief Alignment:** #1 (Built for humans), #3 (No anxiety)  
**Color:** Sand (You Did It!)

**Emotion Target:** Confidence ("This is real and different")

## 🎨 Design System Application

### **Color Strategy**
- **Seafoam (#81C7BD):** Intentionality Check (Do This - present action)
- **Deep Teal (#2F5359):** Joy Buckets, Freedom Timeline (Learn This - understanding)
- **Sand (#D4C4B0):** Coast Mode (You Did It! - celebration)
- **Warm Grays:** Foundation, body text, backgrounds

### **Typography (Jost)**
- Hero: Large, calm, breathing room
- Body: Plain language, scannable
- No jargon, no shame, no red arrows

### **Photography**
- Use Unsplash for authentic moments
- Focus on human connection, not luxury
- Calm, natural lighting
- Depth of moments over height of balances

## 💬 Messaging Principles

### **Voice:**
- Calm, not preachy
- Empowering, not prescriptive
- Clear, not complex
- Human, not calculator

### **Avoid:**
- ❌ "Budget" (use "spending plan")
- ❌ "Sacrifice" (use "focus")
- ❌ "Discipline" (use "clarity")
- ❌ Red arrows, shame, anxiety
- ❌ Dollar amounts in headlines (use years/time)

### **Embrace:**
- ✅ Years, not dollars
- ✅ Freedom, not deprivation
- ✅ Clarity, not complexity
- ✅ Progress, not perfection
- ✅ Moments, not balances

## 🎓 Legal/Liability Considerations

**Critical:** Emphasize "we teach, not prescribe"

**Messaging:**
- "We show you the path" (not "we tell you what to do")
- "We teach principles" (not "we give financial advice")
- "You choose your freedom age" (not "we recommend age X")
- "Adjust your savings rate" (empowerment, not prescription)

**Where to reinforce:**
- Freedom Timeline copy: "You set the target, we show the path"
- Footer: "For educational purposes. Not financial advice."
- About page: Core belief #1 (built for humans = you decide)
